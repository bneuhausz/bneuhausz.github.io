Privacy Policy

Website Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to collect and analyze anonymous, aggregate statistics about our website's traffic.
This service is privacy-focused and does not use cookies or collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
It helps us understand how our site is being used so we can improve the visitor experience, without compromising user privacy.

Newsletter

If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, we will collect your email address to send you content and updates.
We use MailerLite as our email marketing platform to manage our subscriber list and send emails on our behalf.
Your email address will be stored on MailerLite's servers.
You can review MailerLite's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.
You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link provided in the footer of every email.
